# PRIVACY POLICY & CONSUMER HEALTH NOTICE
**Last Updated:** December 24, 2025

**1. INTRODUCTION**
La Compagnie Vaylen Inc. ("Vaylen") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy details how we collect, use, and safeguard your data, including Consumer Health Data, in compliance with Quebec Law 25, GDPR, CCPA, and app store policies.

**2. QUEBEC LAW 25 COMPLIANCE**
If you reside in Quebec:
* **Privacy Officer:** The person in charge of the protection of personal information is the President of La Compagnie Vaylen Inc.
* **Contact:** You may contact the Privacy Officer regarding your data at **legal@vaylen.app**.
* **Data Transfer:** Your personal information is stored on secure cloud servers (e.g., Google Firebase, AWS) which may be located outside of Quebec. By using the Service, you consent to this transfer.

**3. DATA WE COLLECT**
* **3.1 Identity Data:** Name, email address, shipping address (for merch).
* **3.2 Health & Fitness Data:** Weight, height, body metrics, workout logs, nutritional intake, and activity levels.
* **3.3 Device Data:** IP address, device model, OS version, and general location (city/country level).
* **3.4 User Content:** Photos or text you voluntarily upload to the app (e.g., via Camera access).

**4. HOW WE USE YOUR DATA**
We use your data strictly to:
* Provide personalized workout and nutrition plans.
* Process transactions for subscriptions and merchandise.
* Analyze app performance using tools like Google Analytics and Firebase.
* Send you service updates or marketing emails (via Mailchimp), which you can opt-out of at any time.

**5. HEALTHKIT & GOOGLE HEALTH CONNECT (CRITICAL)**
Vaylen integrates with Apple HealthKit and Google Health Connect.
* **No Advertising:** We **do not** use data gained through these frameworks for advertising or marketing purposes.
* **No Data Sales:** We **do not** sell data gained through these frameworks to data brokers or resellers.
* **Usage:** We access this data solely to visualize your fitness progress and calculate metabolic needs within the app.

**6. DATA SHARING & THIRD PARTIES**
We do not sell your personal data. We share data only with third-party service providers necessary to operate our business, including but not limited to:
* **Cloud Hosting:** Firebase, Google Cloud.
* **Payment Processors:** Stripe, Apple Pay, Google Pay.
* **Analytics:** Google Analytics.
* **Marketing:** Mailchimp, Google Ads, Meta Pixel (for website retargeting).
These providers are contractually obligated to protect your data.

**7. YOUR RIGHTS (GDPR / CCPA / LAW 25)**
Depending on your location, you have the right to:
* **Access:** Request a copy of the data we hold about you.
* **Deletion:** Request the permanent deletion of your account and data.
* **Correction:** Update inaccurate information.
To exercise these rights, email **legal@vaylen.app**.

**8. CHILDREN'S PRIVACY**
Vaylen is intended for users aged 18 and older. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

**9. COOKIES**
Our website (vaylen.app) uses cookies to improve user experience and analyze traffic. You can control cookie settings via your browser.

**10. CONTACT**
For privacy concerns: **legal@vaylen.app**
